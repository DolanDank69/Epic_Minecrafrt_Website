What Community

This page of the website is all about recognizing the amazing things that the vast Minecraft community has done.

This will involve complex redstone circuits, world records and anything that the Minecraft community has accomplished.


Zombie Siege By FVDisco

If you are into fun minigames then you will love Zombie Siege by FVDisco. The aim of the game is to kill as many zombies as possible before they reach the players end of the platform.

Atari 2600 Emulator By SethBling

Here we have a working emulation of an Atari 2600. This a complex redstone computer and 2000 command blocks and houses multiple games using dirt blocks a zeros and stone blocks are ones. The creator SethBling has done an amazing job creating a set of games inside of a single game.
